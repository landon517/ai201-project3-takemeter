r/letstalkmusic
what is it: It's a reddit community committed to discussion and discourse of music from a variety of backgrounds and interests. It's holds a large, varied community, supporting discussion on nearly all
fronts of music. Posts are labeled with either hot_take or analysis (a claim with some kind of reasoning meant to spark dicsourse). The distinction matters because
the community values meaningful and rich discourse, often being enlightened


Labels - 
hot take: a claim without any real evidence, grounded in a feeling the author holds, usually leading to discussion. This usually intends to find people with similar opinions.
analysis: a claim backed with evidence usually leading to some kind of discourse. This usually intends to change people's minds.
question: not presented as a claim, but a pursuit of knowledge and perspective. Often seeking opinions of more experienced redditors.


This distinction matters because the community values discourse where new opinions and viewpoints are shared, and people become more aware of the broader musical landscape.
